# DA401 Capstone Project - Seo

## DA Capstone
Formula 1 is complex motorsport with myriad of factors that affects driver and team performance. As part of the Data Analytics capstone project, I will be analyzing the effect of the 2022 FIA regulations on race dynamics with Machine Learning. 

## Research Question
What are the distinguishing characteristics of the races that occurred after a major regulatory change compared to those that occurred before?

## Data Source
Final Race (Q1-Q3 excluded) data from 2019-2025 will be obtained using FasF1 API.

## Methods
Linear Mixed-Effects Models and Random Forrest Classification using R and Python.

## Expected Timeline
13 weeks total
